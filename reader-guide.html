Edu Ebook Reader — how to use it

A local‑first Progressive Web App that opens educational ebooks and runs their interactive and 3D content, offline and on the device.

▶ Open the ReaderFull manual

Six steps

Each step takes a few seconds.

1 · Open the Reader

It runs in the browser, works offline and can be installed as an app (Add to Home Screen / Install). No account, no sign‑in.

2 · Open a file

Choose Open book and pick a local file. Supported: EPUB / EPUB 3, PDF, TXT, Markdown, HTML/XHTML, DOCX, ODT, FB2, CBZ. Your file stays on your device — it is never uploaded.

3 · Read & navigate

Use the table of contents to jump between chapters. The reader remembers your last position for each file.

4 · Interact

Open the demonstration EPUB to try the interactive objects: rotate, zoom and inspect 3D molecular models in fullscreen, play short videos, and answer the embedded quiz.

5 · Customise reading

Adjust font, size, theme, line spacing, text width, letter spacing and reduced motion; use read‑aloud where the browser supports it; switch interface language (English / Greek).

6 · DRM is refused, not bypassed

If a file carries DRM or encryption markers, the reader detects them and refuses to open it. The reader contains no decryption and circumvents no protection.

Tip: for the full interactive experience, open it over HTTPS (this preview is served over HTTPS) on a device with WebGL support.
