Interactive EPUB 3: sp hybridisation in ethyne

A worked example of the kind of interactive learning resource the toolchain produces and reads.

What it contains

Content

  • Explanatory chapters on hybridisation and sigma/pi bonding.
  • Short videos.
  • Multiple interactive 3D viewers: rotate, zoom and fullscreen inspection of sp hybrid orbitals, sigma/pi symmetry and the complete sigma–pi structure of ethyne.
  • Original comprehension questions implemented inside the EPUB.

The science (correct & concise)

  • In sp hybridisation, one s and one p orbital combine into two equivalent sp hybrids.
  • The two sp hybrids point in opposite directions → a linear, 180° geometry.
  • Ethyne (C₂H₂, H–C≡C–H) is linear; the C–C triple bond is one σ bond plus two mutually perpendicular π bonds.
  • π bonds form by sideways orbital overlap, generally weaker than the head‑on overlap of a σ bond.
How the 3D was made: the models were created in a 3D authoring application, exported to glTF/GLB‑compatible assets, and rendered in the browser with a WebGL engine, bundled locally so the scenes work fully offline. The demonstration content is licensed CC BY 4.0.
