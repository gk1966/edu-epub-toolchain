# Legal note

Edu Ebook Reader is an open-source reading application created for educational and
research purposes. This note is informational and is not legal advice.

## Technological protection measures (DRM)
The reader does **not** circumvent, remove or bypass any technological protection
measure. EPUB files that carry DRM / encryption markers — for example
`META-INF/rights.xml` (Adobe ADEPT), a Readium LCP `license.lcpl`, or a
`META-INF/encryption.xml` indicating encrypted content (as opposed to font
obfuscation) — are detected and **refused**. The software contains no decryption
capability for protected content.

This scope is consistent with the principle that technological protection measures
must not be circumvented. Relevant sources include Directive 2001/29/EC, Article 6,
and Greek Law 2121/1993, Article 66A. This short note is not a legal opinion.

## Accessibility
The interface includes accessibility-oriented design features. The project has not
been independently certified for WCAG or European Accessibility Act conformance.
